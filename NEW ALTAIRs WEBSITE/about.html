About us

Altair Retro Ltd, a Kenyan ICT company founded in 2019, bridges the gap by delivering cutting-edge Software Solutions in the Private and Public Sector, ensuring affordability and easy support.
We are an ICT company, located in Nairobi, Kenya. We deal with software development which includes; Accounting software, NGO Information Management Systems, Human resource Software, Procurement Software, Staff Portals, Self-Service Portals, Payroll Software, EDMS, CRM and Microsoft ERPs.
We implement Microsoft Dynamics 365 BC for various industries, including financial institutions and manufacturing.
