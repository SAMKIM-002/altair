Our Inventory System

At Altair Retro, we understand the complexities of managing inventory. Our user-friendly Stores System empowers businesses of all sizes to gain real-time visibility, streamline processes, and make informed stock management decisions. Effortlessly manage your entire inventory lifecycle, from procurement to stock movement and reordering. Boost efficiency, minimize stockouts, and gain valuable insights with our user-friendly platform. Request a free demo today and see how Altair Retro can transform your inventory control.


This is a system that manages the procured goods that are in our stores. It gives us notifications on re-order levels as well as the inventory in the stores.
It has the following modules:

Finance

Allocate budgets for inventory purchases, track inventory value as an asset, manage bank accounts for supplier payments, and generate financial reports that reflect inventory costs.

Procurement and Stock Accounting

Streamline the procurement process for ordering inventory, manage inventory coding for efficient categorization and tracking, and ensure proper accounting practices for stock purchases.

Coding Management

Create and manage inventory-specific codes for efficient stock identification, categorization, and tracking within the system.

Transportation and Tracking

Track the movement of inventory during transportation from suppliers or between warehouses, ensuring accurate stock levels at different locations.

Obsolete, Surplus and Scrap Management

Identify and manage obsolete or surplus inventory, optimize stock levels by removing these items, and potentially manage scrap materials for responsible disposal.
