Streamline Your NGO Operations with
Our Innovative Software

At Altair Retro, we are passionate about supporting the incredible work that NGOs do around the world. We understand the unique challenges you face, from limited resources to complex program management.
That's why we developed our industry-leading NGO Management System, a comprehensive software solution designed to streamline your operations, maximize your impact, and empower you to achieve your mission.


All the NGO functions and operations are taken care of through the following modules:

Grant management

To manage all the grants to the NGO and generate reports on the same.

Donor fund management

Manages all the donor fund by donors and gives all the reports concerning the project being funded, reports available for full sponsorship of a project or partial sponsorship of a project.

Project management

Covers proposal writing, project planning and execution and project reporting and project online collaboration

Finance and operations

Covers all the financial activities from petty cash, assets, banks and financial reporting.

Staff portal

manages all the activities relating to employees, from pays lips, online leave application and approval, p9 for returns to clearance.
