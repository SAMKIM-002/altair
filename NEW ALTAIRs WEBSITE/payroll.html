Our Payroll System

Take control of your payroll and empower your HR team with Altair Retro's innovative Payroll System. Our feature-rich platform equips businesses with the tools they need to automate tasks, minimize errors, and ensure on-time payments for a happy workforce. Simplify payroll processing, boost HR efficiency, and focus on what matters most. Explore our features and discover how Altair Retro can empower your people and simplify payroll.


This is a system that deals with employee payments and statutory management as well as imprest advanced to employees.
It has the following submodules:

Payroll & Payslip Generation

Generate payslips electronically, manage salaries, deductions, and taxes with efficiency and accuracy.

Imprest Surrender

Manage and track imprest funds (cash advances) for employees effectively, ensuring proper record-keeping and accountability.

Statutory Management

Simplify statutory deductions and filings, ensuring compliance with tax regulations and social security contributions.

Timesheets

Manage employee timesheets electronically for accurate payroll calculations and improved transparency.

Staff Portal

Empower employees with a self-service portal to access payslips, request leave (if applicable), and update personal information.
